Mad.co is the MyArtistDNA brand home that explains the related suite of sites: mad.com, mad.tv, mad.fm, and mad.is.

MyArtistDNA is a brand and community for artists to display, organize, monetize and manage their rich media content to increase fan engagement and brand loyalty. The MAD sites empower artists to be heard, be seen, and be independent, by giving them the tools, technology and outlet they need to create a cohesive and powerful web presence. We’ve created our sites to give users maximum control over their brand, product, distribution, and online identity, with design that’s both beautiful and functional.
