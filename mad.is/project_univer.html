Universal Records

When putting together a series of Flash ads for some of Universal Records' upcoming albums, we needed to work with each artists' existing branding and create short, captivating designs that would promote fan attention. For Babyface's "Playlist," we used images that captured the heartfelt sound of the album and included bold lettering to set off the details about the release, and the Flash ad for Will.I.Am's "Songs about Girls" featured his trademark logo and minimal text in order to give the album and the single full attention. The ad for U2 was promoting their 3D concert, and so we used moving text to emulate the 3D experience and flashed quick shots of the band in concert to generate excitement for the show.
