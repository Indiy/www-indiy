JIM JONES

When Jim Jones was preparing to release his new album, "Pray IV Reign," he wanted a website that would put all the media, album and tour information in one place, and that would integrate his existing social media profiles. We gave him a site with a dark, edgy design, and used the distinctive crown from his logo throughout the site for accents of color and to reinforce his personal brand. A high impact homepage provided crucial details and a countdown clock for the album, and a bold text menu led to photos, videos, forums and more.
