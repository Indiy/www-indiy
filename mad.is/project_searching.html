SEARching 4 an outlet

To get fans involved and invested in Young Chris's new album, we put together "Searching for an Outlet," an interactive campaign that invited fans to record a verse for one of Young Chris's new songs. The winning verse was featured on his mix tape, and the ten finalists hosted on the "Searching for an Outlet" website. The contest generated a new fan base for Young Chris, and created a community of listeners around the album.
