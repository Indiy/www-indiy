What is MyArtistDNA?
A home for artists' brands and discovery destination for fan's. MyArtistDNA is a movement that puts the control of the artists back into artist to inspire to be independent. We provide the outlet for Artists' brands to build the way they intended. We are your partner in this journey. Be heard. Be seen. Be independent.

How much does MyArtistDNA cost?
Nothing if you choose us as your partner. We call this the OPT IN option. By opting in we will guide you down this success path and equip you with the tools to succeed. You can OPT OUT. OPT OUT is an option that you pay a monthly fee to use the software and gives you the software with limited features to deploy.

I already have a site, how can I integrate it with MyArtistDNA?
