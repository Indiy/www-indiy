Def Jam

Def Jam came to us to produce a series of microsites and banner ads for some of their upcoming big releases--Kanye, Jay Z, Jagged Edge, the Dream, and others. We created a customized strategy for each artist, creating simple, eye catching ads that linked to focused sites with chat rooms, group listening parties, links to merch, and more. The designs picked up on the album art and reinforced Def Jam's other marketing efforts.
