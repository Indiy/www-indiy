SSWIZZ BEATZ Web Site

Swizz Beats had so much material to showcase and such a variety of work to be highlighted that we knew we needed to keep the web design simple so that his work could speak for itself. A minimalistic splash page showcased some of his most successful projects, and included his trademark triple chevron logo. The site itself was similarly focused, with tiled album covers that opened up to give specific information on the artist, including video and links to audio.
