NIghts of legends

Nights of Legends was a one night televised event featuring a cast of artists that rose to fame in the 50s and 60s. They came to us for branding and web design that would appeal both to long time fans and to new listeners. We opted for a retro logo counterbalanced by a sleek design and blocky fonts, and we created separate pages for each artist, featuring videos, photos, and bios.
