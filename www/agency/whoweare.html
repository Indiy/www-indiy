GET TO KNOW US

MyArtistDNA is a full service strategic marketing and branding company for established and aspiring artists. We get our inspiration from the artists we represent, and we're devoted to creating original campaigns to help take our clients to the next level. We don't just think outside the box-we left the box behind. From logo design to website creation, from social media management to video production, we harness the latest technology to build you a cutting edge, customized internet strategy, and we use an efficient approach that maximizes productivity and minimizes wasted effort or expense. And we're willing to take risks-if you're game to experiment with new methods, so are we. Go ahead-let us get into your DNA.
