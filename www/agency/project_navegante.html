Navegante WEB SITE

Navegante had an edgy, electro sound, and we used that as the inspiration for their colorful, smoky logo and for their website design, which balances translucence and darkness with shots of color and light. The vertically oriented site has a built in video and audio player, and we used Ajax with JQuery to make these run smoothly and to increase the website's usability. The site also incorporated and linked fluidly into the band's Facebook and Twitter accounts, which we branded to reinforce the band's identity.
