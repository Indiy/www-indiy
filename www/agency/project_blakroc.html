BLAKROC WEB SITE

Finding the right branding and identity for a single artist is one thing--creating it for a range of eleven very different musicians is another. To achieve this for the Black Keys' collaborative album BlakRoc, we went with a clean, simple logo, a sans serif type, and a classic black and white design. The site highlighted the various artists involved, and incorporated webisodes that the Black Keys had created as they recorded the album.
