Let's Get Started

1
Add your media
Drag and drop all your files right on to the page (e.g. jpgs, mp4 and mp3).
2
Create your first page
Click on your media files' titles to edit their information and poulate your account.
3
Populate your store!
Add digital or physical goods to your store (e.g. songs, t-shirts, tickets). Set a sale price and shipping fee, and your done.
4
Add your social media feeds
Click on Social Connections to add your Facebook and Twitter account.
5
Create an overlay page
Add whatever information you want. Embed any of your favorite widgets or players into our overlays.
(e.g dropbox, youtube, soundcloud, vimeo, songkick.)
6
Publish your site
Publish and share your page so the world can see your art.
